  1. Truck owner will fill up the registration form of SHOHOZ LTD properly. SHOHOZ authority will use and store all the information.
  2. Truck owner will follow the rules SHOHOZ LTD. If he fails to comply with the rules, SHOHOZ Ltd can terminate the deed anytime. 
  3. From taking over the goods by the driver from the goods owner, starting to reach the owner's destination it will be the responsibility of the driver/ Truck Owner to deliver the goods.
  4. Loader can track his goods from start to end and truck owner can track his truck always by using the VTS/mobile provided by SHOHOZ Ltd.
  5. With the SHOHOZ’s Mobile, the truck owner can track the location of the truck.
  6. SHOHOZ Ltd authority will provide an online platform where truck owner & product sender can easily find each other and can provide and receive services.
  7. At the time of truck registration, SHOHOZ Ltd will check the certificate/license of Driver and Owner and keep the copy of all documents.
  8. SHOHOZ Ltd authority will not be responsible if any truck cancel due to any kind of unwanted events, incidents, weather, political strike, mechanical problem. In that case, SHOHOZ Ltd will try to arrange alternative transport in a short period of time.
  9. SHOHOZ Ltd will not be responsible if the goods face any kind of damage for the negligence of product suppliers and loader or driver.
  10. Truck owner have to make sure that his truck & driver is fully capable of performing the duty. SHOHOZ Ltd authority must be informed as soon as possible with all the updated documents like license, National ID number, Mobile number if the truck owner changes the driver. 
  11. After loading the goods, the truck drivers always have to communicate with the loader. For that, he must be keeping the mobile standby. So that cargo holder & truck owner can communicate if necessary and track the location of the truck.
  12. The truck owner will follow the current law and rules of Bangladesh Govt. and SHOHOZ Ltd
  13. Truck owner or loader can’t change the preferred rental. For the cancelation of trip, they will inform each other as soon as possible. Loader/ Product receivers will unload the product as soon as possible. It will be the responsibility of product receiver to unload the products. Of course if there any kind of direction to unload the product, it will be followed.
  14. SHOHOZ Ltd authority will receive the preferred commission. If anyone cancels the trip in the last moment, he will be penalized.
  15. This deed will be effective till any problem arise. Terms and conditions of this deed can be changed with mutual agreement of both parties.
  16. If any truck owner or driver break the law of Bangladesh Govt, then the deal will be cancel without any prior notice.
  17. SHOHOZ Ltd will not be accountable for carrying any kind of illegal product, activities or mislead any information.
  18. If product sender has insurance of the product and if it faces any kind of accident, then he will get the compensation of according to insurance policy. In this case, he must be following the rules of insurance company.
  19. In case of any dispute it will be solved with mutual discussion by involved parties. Otherwise it will be settled with the help of existing laws of Bangladesh Government.
  20. At the time of SHOHOZ trip, all the documents of vehicle and driver should be updated and must be run by the traffic rules of Bangladesh.
  21. If any registered vehicles or truck driver get into accident, then after getting rescue call from them SHOHOZ Ltd will send support and rescue team as soon as possible to provide all necessary support. Truck owner will bear all the logical charges regarding this.
  22. If Transport Company nominates any truck, then that transport company must take the responsibility of verifying all documents regarding vehicle and driver. And it must be responsible for activities of them according to SHOHOZ Ltd
